Rock The Port Music Festival — October 10 – 12, Hamburg

Get ready for a music extravaganza like no other! Come to the Hamburger Port for an Autumn weekend of non-stop live music, delicious food, and endless fun. With a diverse lineup of local and international acts, from indie rock to hip-hop, pop to electronic, our festival will have you dancing the night away. And with the stunning waterfront setting, you'll have the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable experience. Don't miss out on the party of the year! Mark your calendars and grab your tickets now!
